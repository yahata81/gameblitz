Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

GameBlitz ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information in our mobile application GameBlitz (the "App"), which is designed for children ages 8-12.

1. COPPA Compliance

GameBlitz complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Our App is designed to operate without requiring any personal information from its users.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or physical addresses
  • Phone numbers or other contact information
  • Ages or dates of birth
  • Photos, videos, or audio recordings
  • Precise geolocation data
  • Social media account information
  • The text of game ideas entered by users (see Section 3)

3. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our service:

  • Anonymous Device Identifier: A randomly generated identifier stored locally on the device to associate game creations with a device. This is not linked to any personal identity.
  • Structured Game Data: When a user creates a game, we store structured metadata only (game mode, variant, theme keywords, and emoji selections). We do not store the raw text input provided by the user.
  • Subscription Status: If a purchase is made, we store the subscription tier (free, pro, or max) associated with the anonymous device identifier. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store; we do not collect or store any payment information.
  • Usage Analytics: Play counts for generated games, stored anonymously.

4. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used solely to:

  • Generate and store games created by users
  • Track remaining game creation slots (free tier limits)
  • Manage subscription status
  • Improve the quality of game generation

5. Content Moderation

All user input is filtered through a content moderation system before being processed by our AI game generation engine. This system blocks inappropriate or violent content to ensure a safe experience for children.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Cloud database and serverless functions for game generation and storage. Data is stored securely and encrypted at rest.
  • AI Language Model: We use AI services to interpret game ideas and generate game configurations. Only structured prompts are sent to the AI; raw user input text is never stored.
  • Apple App Store / RevenueCat: Subscription management and payment processing. All payment data is handled by Apple; we never see or store credit card information.

These services process data in accordance with their own privacy policies. No personal information about children is shared with these services.

7. Parental Gate

All in-app purchases require parental verification through a math-based challenge (Parental Gate) before any transaction can be initiated. This is designed to prevent unauthorized purchases by children.

8. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored on secure, encrypted servers provided by Supabase. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the limited data we collect. Since we do not collect personal information, the risk of data exposure is minimal.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

Game data is retained as long as the App is installed on the device. Users can delete individual games from within the App at any time. Uninstalling the App removes the local device identifier; associated server data may be retained for a reasonable period before automatic deletion.

10. Parents' Rights

Since we do not collect personal information from children, there is generally no personal data to review, modify, or delete. However, if you believe we have inadvertently collected any personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
